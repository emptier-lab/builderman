Changelog

Track all updates and improvements to the incorp bot.

Mines Gambling Game

v2.2.4

Introducing our new high-risk, high-reward Mines gambling game where players strategically reveal cells while avoiding hidden mines to win big.

  • Added Mines gambling game
  • New command: .mines to play the mines game
  • New command: .reveal to uncover cells in mines
  • New command: .cashout to collect winnings

Ladders Multiplayer Game

v2.2.3

Expanded our multiplayer offerings with the Ladders game, allowing up to 4 players to compete simultaneously in a climbing challenge with progressive difficulty and rewards.

  • Added Ladders multiplayer gambling game
  • New command: .ladders to start a ladder climbing game
  • New command: .enter ladders to join existing games
  • Implemented multiplayer support for up to 4 players
  • Created progressive difficulty with increasing rewards

Plinko Gambling Game

v2.2.2

Added the classic Plinko gambling game with animated ASCII visualization, variable risk levels, and integration with our cooldown reducer system for enhanced gameplay.

  • Added Plinko gambling game
  • New command: .plinko to play with varying payouts
  • Implemented animated ASCII visualization
  • Added support for all/half bet amounts
  • Integrated with cooldown reducer system
  • Enhanced gambling options with risk-based rewards

Home Market System

v2.2.1

Launched our comprehensive Home Market System allowing players to purchase, manage, and sell virtual properties with maintenance requirements and a new food system for enhanced social gameplay.

  • Introduced Home Market System
  • New command: .homes to browse available homes
  • New command: .buyhome to purchase properties
  • New command: .myhome to view your home details
  • New command: .sellhome to sell your property
  • New command: .paybills for weekly home maintenance
  • New command: .bills to view your home maintenance
  • New command: .removeguest to remove a guest from your home
  • Added Food System with .grocery, .buyfood and .myfood
  • Enhanced social features with .invite command
